Your participant data lives in Salesforce, Google Sheets, WhatsApp, email inboxes, and someone's laptop. By the time you compile a funder report, the data is three months old.

Gateway was built because we faced this exact problem — running a multi-country programme with no purpose-built infrastructure. We built the system we wished existed, and it can be customised for yours.

Platform Capabilities

One platform for your entire programme lifecycle

Stop stitching together 10 tools. Gateway handles selection, management, engagement, evaluation, and reporting — and adapts to how your programme actually works.

01

Select the Right Participants at Scale

Run competitive application cycles that can handle tens of thousands of applicants. Multi-section forms, eligibility screening, document and video uploads, AI-assisted scoring, and structured multi-reviewer workflows — all in one place.

  • Process 50,000+ applications per cycle without manual bottlenecks
  • AI reads and pre-scores essays so reviewers focus on the best candidates
  • Two-stage selection: shortlisting through final decisions with full audit trails
  • Applicants can save progress, resume via magic link, and upload from any device
📋
50K+
Applications per cycle. No bottlenecks.
02

Track Outcomes, Not Just Activities

Move beyond counting attendance. Gateway collects surveys, polls, and participation data — then surfaces trends through dashboards your team and funders can access in real time. Ask questions about your data in plain English.

  • Surveys and polls with automated collection and trend analysis
  • Track attendance, milestones, and completion rates across cohorts
  • AI-powered natural language queries — "What percentage of women completed Module 3?"
  • Generate funder-ready M&E reports in minutes, not weeks
  • Import your historical data to track growth over years

Enable Peer Learning & Collaboration

Participants don't just consume — they contribute. Gateway supports project submissions, structured peer review, thematic working groups, and cross-partner knowledge exchange.

  • Participants submit community projects linked to SDGs and local outcomes
  • Structured peer review matched by topic, region, or institution
  • Thematic working groups for focused collaboration
  • Cross-institutional learning across dozens of partner organisations

04

Bring Your Partners Into One System

Whether you work with universities, regional offices, government agencies, or community organisations — Gateway gives each partner a scoped view of their own participants while keeping programme-level data unified for your central team.

  • Partner-specific roles with institution-scoped access and permissions
  • Verification workflows so partners confirm enrolment and eligibility
  • Multi-country, multi-partner cohort management from one dashboard
  • Partner-level reporting without exposing data from other institutions

Engage Thousands Without a Comms Team

  • Bulk email with open-rate tracking, segmented by cohort, region, or group

  • Centralised document library with role-based access

Intelligent Monitoring & Evaluation

  • AI essay scoring evaluates thousands of applications in minutes
  • Multi-source chatbot queries polls, documents, and programme data simultaneously
  • "How are refugee participants performing compared to the cohort average?" — just ask
  • Demographic disaggregation by gender, disability, refugee status, and more

Generate Funder Reports in Minutes

All your data — applications, participation, surveys, outcomes — lives in one system. When it's reporting time, the data is already clean, disaggregated, and ready.

  • Interactive dashboards your funders can access directly
  • SDG-aligned outcome tracking and disaggregated demographics
  • Export to CSV, PDF, or connect to external evaluation tools
  • Full audit trail for compliance and accountability
  • Track participant growth across years and cohorts

Enterprise Security, Purpose-Built for Development

Your participants' data deserves the same protection as any enterprise system. Gateway meets the requirements of institutional partners like Tufts University and Mastercard Foundation.

  • Role-based access control with granular permissions and group inheritance
  • Federated authentication via Google and Microsoft
  • GDPR-compliant logging — no IP addresses, no fingerprinting
  • Cloud-hosted infrastructure with enterprise-grade encryption
  • Further customisable to your programme's security requirements
