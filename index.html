Platform Capabilities

Everything you need to run youth development at scale

From application intake to impact reporting, Gateway replaces spreadsheets, shared drives, and disconnected tools with a single purpose-built platform.

📋

Applications & Selection

Multi-section application forms with eligibility screening, AI-assisted essay scoring, structured reviewer workflows, and two-stage selection — shortlisting through final decision.

👥

Cohort & Student Management

Manage students across cohorts, working groups, and universities. Track enrollment, demographics, and participation with bulk import/export and individual profiles.

🤖

AI-Powered Chatbot

Multi-agent AI assistant that queries poll data, searches documents, and surfaces WordPress content — giving program staff instant answers from their own data.

📊

Analytics & Dashboards

Embedded Superset dashboards with real-time analytics. Track engagement, demographics, poll responses, and program outcomes across your entire portfolio.

📨

Communications

Bulk email with rich text editing, open-rate tracking, and targeted sending by cohort, working group, or custom segments. Keep your community informed and engaged.

🔐

Security & Access Control

Enterprise-grade RBAC with granular permissions, group inheritance, and dual OAuth (Google + Microsoft). GDPR-compliant logging and Tufts-standard security.

The Platform Journey

From application to impact, in one system

Gateway manages the full lifecycle of youth development programs — replacing fragmented tools with a unified workflow.

01

Apply

Students submit multi-section applications with essays, documents, and video — accessible from any device, embeddable on your website.

02

Review & Select

AI pre-scores essays while human reviewers apply structured rubrics. Two-stage selection with bulk operations and audit trails.

03

Manage & Engage

Onboard students into cohorts and working groups. Track participation, send targeted communications, and manage documents.

04

Measure Impact

Collect poll data, analyze trends with AI, and generate funder-ready reports through embedded dashboards and analytics.
